## Specific Checks (By Fuel Type)

### Electrical Lifts
*   **Battery:** Cables intact? No corrosion? Vent caps in place?
*   **Plug:** Connector not damaged?
*   **Water:** Level ok? (Check only after charging).

### Propane (LPG) / Gas Cylinder
*   **Tank:** Locked down securely? No rust/dents?
*   **Hose:** No hissing sound or smell of leak?
*   **Connections:** Tight?
