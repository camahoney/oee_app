## Scoring Key
*   **S:** Satisfactory (Performed safely)
*   **U:** Unsatisfactory (Safety violation or poor control)

## The Road Test

### 1. Pre-Operational Check
*   [ ] **Walk-Around:** Checked tires, leaks, guard, forks.
*   [ ] **Controls:** Checked horn, lights, brakes, steering smooth.
*   [ ] **Seatbelt:** Fastened *immediately* upon sitting down.

### 2. Traveling (Unloaded)
*   [ ] **Forks:** Kept low (4-6 inches) and tilted back.
*   [ ] **Speed:** Controlled, safe speed suitable for environment.
*   [ ] **Intersections:** Slowed down, honked horn, looked both ways.
*   [ ] **Mirrors:** Checked rear swing before turning.

### 3. Load Handling (Stacking/Unstacking)
*   [ ] **Approach:** Squared up to the load/rack.
*   [ ] **Engagement:** Forks centered and fully inserted.
*   [ ] **Lifting:** Lifted load smoothly; checked overhead clearance.
*   [ ] **Traveling Loaded:** Drove backwards if view obstructed.
*   [ ] **Placement:** Lowered load smoothly; checked stability before backing away.

### 4. Parking & Shut Down
*   [ ] **Location:** Parked in designated safe area (not blocking aisle/exit).
*   [ ] **Forks:** Lowered flush to the floor (tripping hazard removed).
*   [ ] **Brake:** Parking brake applied.
*   [ ] **Power:** Key turned off and removed.

---

## Critical Failures (Automatic Fail)
*Did the operator do any of the following?*
*   [ ] Hit a rack or object.
*   [ ] Traveled with forks high (>12 inches).
*   [ ] Failed to wear seatbelt.
*   [ ] Spun wheels or "hot rodded".

## Certification Decision
*   [ ] **PASS:** Operator is competent to operate this lift truck.
*   [ ] **FAIL:** Operator requires more practice and re-evaluation.
