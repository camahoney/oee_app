TPM Fundamentals

Total Productive Maintenance

Vetted Safe | Continuous Improvement Series

Learning Objective

"To treat your machine like you treat your car. Don't drive it until the wheels fall off."

Shared Ownership

It is not "Operations vs Maintenance". It is "We own this machine together".

The 3 Zeroes

  • Zero Breakdowns: Fix small things before they break.
  • Zero Defects: A healthy machine makes good parts.
  • Zero Accidents: A clean machine is a safe machine.

C.L.I.T.

The Daily Routine for Operators:

  • C - Clean (Inspect through cleaning).
  • L - Lubricate (Oil is life).
  • I - Inspect (Look for loose bolts).
  • T - Tighten (Fix the rattle).
