Operator Care
🛠
The Golden Rule: Treat the machine like you own it. If you take care of it, it will take care of you.

Operator Care (C.L.I.)

C - Clean
Clean to Inspect

Wipe off grease/dust. Look for cracks or leaks while you do it.

L - Lube
Fight Friction

Check oil levels. Grease bearings. Friction kills machines.

I - Inspect
Tighten Bolts

Vibration loosens everything. Check your witness marks.

OEE Killers

Machine Availability x Performance x Quality

Micro-Stops:
Those little jams that happen every 5 minutes? They kill efficiency more than big breakdowns.

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Mark gauges with "Green Zones".
  • Listen for new noises.
  • Tag abnormalities immediately.
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO ignoring oil leaks.
  • NO running machines "dry".
  • NO waiting for maintenance to flip a switch.
