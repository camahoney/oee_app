The Golden Rule: You cannot improve what you do not measure. Know the score, and play to win.

The Scoreboard (S.Q.D.C.)

S - Safety
Are we Safe?

Accidents, Near Misses, Hazards Reported. If this is Red, nothing else matters.

Q - Quality
Are we Good?

Scrap Rate, Defects, Customer Claims. Do it right the first time.

D - Delivery
Are we On Time?

Parts per Hour, Schedule Adherence. Keep the flow moving.

C - Cost
Are we Efficient?

Overtime, Waste, Material usage. Save pennies to save dollars.

The Feedback Loop

🔄
Action → Result → Adjust

Feedback is the breakfast of champions.

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Check the Scoreboard every shift.
  • Ask "How can I help us win today?"
  • Accept feedback as coaching, not criticism.
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO hiding bad news/data.
  • NO ignoring the targets.
  • NO "It's not my job" attitude.
