Troubleshooting
⚡
The Golden Rule: Respect the Arc Flash. Never open a panel unless you are a qualified electrician wearing arc flash gear.

The Reset Rules

1. Cool Down
Wait 1 Minute

The breaker is hot (thermal overload). Give it time to cool.

2. One Shot
Reset Once

Turn it OFF, then ON. If it holds, good. If it trips again, STOP.

3. Call Pro
Don't Force It

A second trip means a short circuit. Forcing it causes fires.

Look Don't Touch

🚫
480 Volts Kills

Standard industrial power is deadly.

Washdown Tip:
Cover panels with bags before washing machines.

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Check cords for fraying/cuts daily.
  • Report loose wires immediately.
  • Turn head away when resetting breakers.
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO resetting a breaker more than once.
  • NO using duct tape on power cords.
  • NO blocking access to electrical panels (36" clearance).
