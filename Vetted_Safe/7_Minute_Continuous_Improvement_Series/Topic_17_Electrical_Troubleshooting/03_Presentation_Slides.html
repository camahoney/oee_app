Learning Objective

"To respect the invisible boundary. Know what you can touch (Operator Controls) and what kills (Inside the Panel)."

The Danger: Arc Flash

An Arc Flash is 35,000°F. It vaporizes metal. Unless you are wearing this suit, do not open the door.

Troubleshooting: The Breaker

  • Thermal Overload: It trips because it got hot.
  • Rule 1: Wait 1 minute for it to cool.
  • Rule 2: Reset ONCE. If it trips again, STOP.
  • Why? The second trip is usually an explosion.

Lockout / Tagout (LOTO)

Zero Energy State. Control the main power.

Final Review

  • 1. Don't open panels.
  • 2. Reset breakers only once.
  • 3. Keep panels clear (36 inches).
  • 4. Respect the Arc Flash.
