SMED (Changeover)
The Pit Stop Method
⏱
The Golden Rule: Do everything you can while the machine is running (External). Only stop the machine when you absolutely have to (Internal).

The 3 Steps to Speed

1. Separate
Sort the steps

Which steps are Internal (stopped) and which are External (running)?

2. Convert
Move them out

Can we clean the bolts beforehand? Can we pre-heat the mold?

3. Streamline
Make it fast

Use clamps instead of bolts. Use guides instead of measuring.

The Pit Stop

🏁
Preparation is Key

Drivers don't wait for the mechanic to find his wrench.

Goal:
Single Minute (Less than 10 minutes)

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Gather all tools BEFORE stopping the machine.
  • Check the new parts BEFORE removing the old ones.
  • Identify "adjustments" that take too long.
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO looking for tools while stopped.
  • NO leaving the machine area during changeover.
  • NO guessing on settings (Use marks).
