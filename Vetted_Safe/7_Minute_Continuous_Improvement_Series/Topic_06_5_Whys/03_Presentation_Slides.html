5 Whys Analysis

Root Cause Problem Solving

Vetted Safe | Continuous Improvement Series

Learning Objective

"To stop fixing the symptom (The Puddle) and start fixing the root cause (The Leaking Seal)."

The Iceberg Model

Above water: What we see (The Problem). Below water: Why it happened (The Cause).

The Jefferson Memorial Example

  • Problem: Stone is eroding. Why?
  • Why? Frequent washing.
  • Why? Bird poop.
  • Why? Spiders (Birds eat spiders).
  • Why? Midges (Spiders eat midges).
  • Root Cause: Lights turn on at dusk. (Turn them on 1 hour later).

The Trap: Blaming People

If your 5th Why is "Human Error", you failed.

  • People make mistakes because the Process allows it.
  • Ask: Why did the process let him make a mistake?
