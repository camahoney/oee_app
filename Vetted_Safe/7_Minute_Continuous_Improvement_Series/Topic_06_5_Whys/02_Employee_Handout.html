5 Whys Analysis
Root Cause Problem Solving
❓
The Golden Rule: "Human Error" is not a root cause. It is a starting point. Keep asking why until you find the process failure.

The 5 Steps Down

1. Why?
The machine stopped. (Fuse blew).
2. Why?
The motor overheated.
3. Why?
There was no oil in the shaft.
4. Why?
The oil seal was leaking.
5. Why?
ROOT CAUSE: We bought cheap seals that don't match the spec.

The Band-Aid Trap

🩹
Replacing the Fuse?

If you just replace the fuse (Why #1), it will blow again tomorrow.

Fix the Cheap Seal.

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Go to the 'Gemba' (the spot) to see facts.
  • Ask "Why" until causes are technical/process based.
  • Test your fix.
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO assuming you know the answer.
  • NO stopping at "Operator Error".
  • NO arguing about opinions. Look at data.
