Team Leadership
Servant Leadership in Manufacturing
👤
Learning Objective: Trainees (esp. Team Leads) will understand that their primary job is to serve the value-adders (operators) by removing obstacles.

Trainer's Script

Minute 0-1 (The 'Why')

Ask: "Who is the most important person in this factory?"

State: "It's not the CEO. It's the Operator touching the part. They are the only ones adding value."

Purpose: "Leadership isn't about power. It's about removing barriers so the Operator can win."

Minute 2-3 (The Inverted Pyramid)

(Draw or show Pyramid). "Traditionally, Boss is at the top."

In Lean: "Flip it. Customers at the top. Operators next. Team Leads under them. Plant Manager at the bottom."

The Job: "The Manager supports the Lead. The Lead supports the Operator. The Operator satisfies the Customer."

Interactive Scenario

The "Water Spider":

"Imagine a surgeon performing heart surgery. Do they stop to go get more gauze? No. A nurse hands it to them."

Analogy: "The Operator is the Surgeon. The Team Lead is the Nurse (Water Spider). Keep the surgeon working."

Minute 4-5 (Daily Management)

1. Gemba Walk: "Go see the process every day. Show respect."

2. Huddle: "Start the shift with a 5-minute standup. Safety, Quality, Delivery."

3. Andon Response: "When a light goes red, run to help. Don't yell."

Minute 6 (Site Specifics)

"Let's discuss our escalation path." (Review sidebar).

"We end with a 10-question quiz."
