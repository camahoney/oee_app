Team Leadership
Support & Respect
👤
The Golden Rule: Lead by serving. If you are not adding value to the product, you must be serving someone who is.

Core Leadership Habits

1. Go See
The Gemba Walk

Don't manage from an office. Go to the floor. Look at the process. Ask questions.

2. Daily Huddle
Standard Comms

Start every shift with the Plan. Review Safety, Quality, Delivery. Get everyone aligned.

3. Respect
Listen First

The operator knows more about the machine than you do. Listen to their ideas.

The Inverted Pyramid

▼
Customer on Top

Operators support Customers. Leaders support Operators.

Servant Leadership
"How can I help you win today?"

Critical Behaviors (for Leaders)

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Ask "What do you think?" before giving orders.
  • Respond quickly to calls for help.
  • Give credit to the team.
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO shooting the messenger.
  • NO managing by fear.
  • NO ignoring safety issues.
