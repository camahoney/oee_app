Autonomous Maint
Step 1: Restoration
🪞
The Golden Rule: Don't just fix it. Find out WHY it got dirty/loose, and fix the source.

Daily Routine

1. Clean
Wipe it Down

Clean hands on the machine helps you feel vibration and heat.

2. Tag
Mark Defects

If you see a leak or crack, put a Red Tag on it so it gets fixed.

3. Match
Match Marks

Check the paint marks on bolts. If they don't line up, it's loose.

The Source

🧹
Stop the Dust

Cleaning dust every day is waste.

Root Cause:
Find where the dust is coming from and seal it. Fix the source.

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Do your AM route at the start of shift.
  • Replace missing bolts immediately.
  • Update the visual standard if you change something.
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO ignoring Red Tags.
  • NO using wire to hold things together (Jury-rigging).
  • NO saying "It's dirty because it's a factory."
