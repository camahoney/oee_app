Inventory & Kanban
Just-In-Time (JIT)
📦
The Golden Rule: No Ticket, No Work. Never produce a part unless you have a signal (Kanban) telling you the customer needs it.

Kanban Basics

1. The Signal
"I need more"

It can be a card, an empty bin, or a designated space. It authorizes you to work.

2. The Supermarket
Visual Stock

A place where standard stock is kept. We refill strictly what was taken.

3. FIFO
First In, First Out

Use the oldest stock first. Prevents parts from expiring or rusting at the back.

The Traffic Light

GREEN: Max Level. STOP. Do not build.

YELLOW: Reorder Point. Prepare to build.

RED: Danger Zone. Build immediately!

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Move the card when you move the bin.
  • Respect the "Max" line.
  • Rotate stock (oldest in front).
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO losing the Kanban cards.
  • NO overloading the bin (overflow).
  • NO building "Just in Case".
