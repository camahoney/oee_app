Machine Basics

The Golden Rule: Never put your hands where your eyes can't see. And never fight a machine; the machine always wins.

The Operator's Code

1. Guarding
The Shield

Guards keep you alive. If a guard is loose or missing, do not run.

2. E-Stop
The Brake

Test it daily. Know exactly where it is so you can hit it blind.

3. Jam Clearing
The Danger Zone

Stop. LOTO. Wait for movement to stop. THEN clear.

Respect the Envelope

🔴
The Red Zone

The machine moves fast and without warning.

Focus:
No phones. No distractions. Watch the point of operation.

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Test E-Stops at start of shift.
  • Wear PPE (Glasses, Earplugs, Shoes).
  • Keep area clean (Trip hazards).
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO bypassing interlocks.
  • NO reaching into a moving machine.
  • NO wearing loose clothing/jewelry.
