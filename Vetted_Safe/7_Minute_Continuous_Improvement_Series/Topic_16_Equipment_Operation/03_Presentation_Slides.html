Respect the Machine

Vetted Safe | Continuous Improvement Series

The Interlock

This switch is the only thing between you and 100 horsepower. Never defeat it with a "cheater key".

The E-Stop

Test it every shift. Know where it is. Hit it HARD if you see trouble.

The Envelope

  • Machines are mindless. They don't have sensors for "Human bone".
  • Guards create a safe perimeter.
  • If a guard is off, the machine is OFF.

Jam Clearing

The #1 cause of hand injuries.

  • Wrong: Reaching in while it's "just paused".
  • Right: Stop -> Lockout -> Clear.
  • Remember: It takes 5 seconds to lock out. It takes forever to grow a finger.
