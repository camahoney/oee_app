Coaching & Feedback
GROW Model
🤝
The Golden Rule: Feedback is the breakfast of champions. Seek it out, listen to it, and act on it.

The G.R.O.W. Model

G - Goal
What do you want?

"I want to learn how to operate the CNC machine."

R - Reality
Where are you?

"I know how to load it, but I don't know the G-Code."

O - Options
What can you do?

"I could read the manual, or ask John to shadow me."

W - Way Forward
What WILL you do?

"I will ask John on Tuesday to teach me."

Active Listening

👂
2 Ears, 1 Mouth

Use them in that proportion.

Don't Reload:
While they are talking, don't think about your response. Just listen.

Critical Behaviors

ALWAYS (DO)
  • Ask "What do you need from me?"
  • Give specific examples (not generalities).
  • Follow up on commitments.
NEVER (DON'T)
  • NO "feedback sandwiches" (Good-Bad-Good). Be direct.
  • NO yelling/shouting.
  • NO gossip. Talk TO people, not ABOUT them.
