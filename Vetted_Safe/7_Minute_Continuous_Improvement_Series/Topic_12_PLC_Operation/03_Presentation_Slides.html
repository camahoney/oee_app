Learning Objective

"To demystify the 'Black Box'. Understand that the PLC only does what its Inputs tell it to do."

Ladder Logic

It reads like a book (Left to Right). Even if the Motor Coil is ON, it won't start if a 'Stop' contact is open.

I - P - O Cycle

  • Input: Sensors, Buttons, Switches (The "Eyes").
  • Process: The CPU/Brain (The "Logic").
  • Output: Lights, Motors, Valves (The "Hands").

Troubleshooting Inputs

If the machine won't start:

  • Check the Sensor Lights.
  • Is the reflector dirty?
  • Is the Limit Switch bent?
  • 90% of PLC problems are actually Field Device problems.
