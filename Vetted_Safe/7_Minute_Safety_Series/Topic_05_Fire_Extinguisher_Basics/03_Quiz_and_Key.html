1. B) Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep.
Rationale: This is the universal standard for operation.

2. C) Base of the fire.
Rationale: You must coat the fuel source to smother the fire.

3. True.
Rationale: Never get trapped. If the fire blocks your exit, you die.

4. B) Evacuate.
Rationale: Smoke inhalation kills more people than flames. If smoke is thick, get out.

5. B) Activate Alarm.
Rationale: Alerting others saves lives. Fighting the fire is secondary.

6. B) 10-15 Seconds.
Rationale: Speed is critical. Don't waste the chemical.

7. False.
Rationale: Fires often flare back up. Back away while watching the fire.

8. B) Wood, Liquids, Electrical.
Rationale: ABC is the "Universal" dry chemical unit.

9. B) Side to side.
Rationale: Cover the entire width of the fire base.

10. Site Specific.
Rationale: Employee must know where to go during a head-count.
