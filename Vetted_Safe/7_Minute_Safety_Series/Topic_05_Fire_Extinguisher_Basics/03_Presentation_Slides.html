Fight or Flee?

Why Train?

  • 90% of fires are small at the start.
  • Panic is the enemy.
  • You need to know your limits.

The Decision

Green = Go. Red = Get Out.

Know Your Enemy

Never put water on a Grease or Electrical Fire.

The Technique

Pull. Aim. Squeeze. Sweep.

Scenario: Office Trash

Small wastebasket fire. No smoke. Exit is clear.

Decision: FIGHT (Green Light)

Scenario: Warehouse Drum

Chemical drum exploded. Smoke at ceiling. 20ft flames.

Decision: FLEE (Red Light)
