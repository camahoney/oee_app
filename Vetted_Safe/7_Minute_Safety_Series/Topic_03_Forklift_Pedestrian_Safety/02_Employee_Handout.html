Hazards

The Triangle of Danger

Drivers cannot see everything. The rear blind spot is massive.

Rear Hazard

When reversing, the driver is looking backwards but the counterweight swings wide. Stay 3ft clear.

Front Hazard

The mast and load block the view. Make sure they see you.
