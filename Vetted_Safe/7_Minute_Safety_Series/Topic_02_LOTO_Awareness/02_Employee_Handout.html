Golden Rule: One Lock. One Key. One Person.

Know Your Role

Affected Employee (You?)

  • You OPERATE the equipment.
  • You are PROTECTED by the locks others apply.
  • You NEVER apply or remove locks.

Authorized Employee (Maintenance)

  • Has specialized training.
  • Has a personal lock labeled with their name.
  • VERIFIES zero energy.

Scenario: The Jam

The Risk: Reaching in to clear a jammed box often releases stored energy. The belt can jump forward instantly.

The Fix:

  • Stop the line.
  • Call Maintenance/Authorized.
  • Wait for Lockout.
  • Verify safe.
