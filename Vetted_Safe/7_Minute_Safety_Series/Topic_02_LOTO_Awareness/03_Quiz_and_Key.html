Trainer Answer Key
RESTRICTED ACCESS
Do not distribute to class.
Remediation: If employee scores <80% (fewer than 8 correct), review the missed questions immediately.

1. C) The Authorized Person.
Rationale: Only the owner of the lock can remove it. Anyone else doing so creates a deadly risk.

2. False.
Rationale: Accidents happen in split seconds. Duration of work does not excuse LOTO.

3. B) You work in the area...
Rationale: Affected employees must recognize LOTO but cannot perform it.

4. B) Call Supervisor.
Rationale: Only Site Management can authorize emergency removal after strict verification.

5. A) The Name.
Rationale: We must know who is in danger if we energize the machine.

6. B) Ensure power is off.
Rationale: Switches can fail. Verification is the final check.

7. False.
Rationale: Tags are easily removed or ignored. Locks provide physical restraint.

8. Yes.
Rationale: Cleaning often requires bypassing guards, which triggers the LOTO standard.

9. A) Red.
Rationale: Standardize on Red for LOTO to distinguish from other locks.

10. False.
Rationale: LOTO locks must be singular in purpose. Using them for toolboxes dilutes their meaning.
