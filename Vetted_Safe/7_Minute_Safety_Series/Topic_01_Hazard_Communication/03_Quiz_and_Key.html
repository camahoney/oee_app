Trainer Answer Key
RESTRICTED ACCESS
Do not distribute to class.
Remediation: If employee scores <80% (fewer than 8 correct), review the missed questions immediately and document the discussion.

1. B) Safety Data Sheet.
Rationale: Updated from "Material Safety Data Sheet" (MSDS) to SDS under GHS.

2. B) Section 8.
Rationale: Section 8 details Exposure Controls and specific PPE requirements.

3. False.
Rationale: Some acids (like Hydrofluoric) or solvents look like water but cause severe burns or absorption hazards.

4. B) DANGER.
Rationale: Danger = Severe. Warning = Moderate.

5. C) Do not use it.
Rationale: Never use an unidentified chemical. It is a severe safety violation.

6. B) Before using...
Rationale: You need to know the hazards *before* exposure.

7. False.
Rationale: Food containers should NEVER be used for chemicals, even if labeled. It creates a poisoning risk.

8. B) Flush for full 15 minutes.
Rationale: Chemical burns continue to damage tissue if not fully diluted/removed.

9. B) The Flame.
Rationale: Universal GHS symbol for fire hazards.

10. Site Specific.
Rationale: Trainee must write the correct location (e.g., "Break room", "Supervisor Office") to pass.
