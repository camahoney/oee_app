My Habits

Staying Safe with Chemicals

Use Required PPE: Every time. Not just when supervisor is looking.
Secondary Containers: Transferring to a spray bottle? You MUST write the Name and Hazard on it.
Personal Hygiene: Wash hands before eating. Never eat/drink in chemical areas.

Emergency Response Plan

If something goes wrong (Leak, Odor, Splash):

  1. Alert: Warn others in the area.
  2. Evacuate: Move to fresh air or safe zone.
  3. Shower/Flush: If on skin/eyes, use the station for 15 mins.
  4. Report: Tell your supervisor immediately.
