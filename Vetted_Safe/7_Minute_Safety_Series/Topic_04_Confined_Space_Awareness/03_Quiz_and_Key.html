Trainer Answer Key
RESTRICTED ACCESS
Do not distribute to class.
Remediation: If employee scores <80% (fewer than 8 correct), review the missed questions immediately.

1. B) Large enough... limited exit.
Rationale: This is the OSHA definition. It captures the difficulty of rescue.

2. B) Rush in without testing.
Rationale: It's human instinct to help, but it creates multiple fatalities.

3. False.
Rationale: CO, Nitrogen, and Methane are odorless. H2S kills your sense of smell instantly.

4. C) Use a tool.
Rationale: Never break the plane of entry with your face/airway.

5. A) Authorized with Permit.
Rationale: Only trained entrants with a signed permit can pass the threshold.

6. B) Putting any part...
Rationale: If you stick your head in to look, you can inhale deadly gas and collapse forward into the hole.

7. True.
Rationale: Rust inside a steel tank can consume all the oxygen, leaving deadly nitrogen air.

8. B) Trained person.
Rationale: The Attendant's sole job is to monitor and call for help. They cannot leave.

9. B) Call for Help.
Rationale: Raise the alarm. Do not become a second victim.

10. True.
Rationale: Deep trenches (over 4ft) are confined spaces with engulfment hazards.
