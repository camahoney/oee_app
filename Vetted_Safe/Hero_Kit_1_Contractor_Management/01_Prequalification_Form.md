# Contractor Prequalification Questionnaire (PQF)
**Version:** 1.0  
**Format:** Word Doc (Editable)  
**Purpose:** To screen contractors for safety performance *prior* to awarding a bid.

---

## [INTERNAL USE ONLY] The "Stop Light" Scoring Guide
*Use this guide to grade the contractor's responses.*

| Score | Rating | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pass** | **Green** | Approved for all work types. |
| **Conditional** | **Yellow** | Approved with extra supervision or PIP (Performance Improvement Plan). |
| **Hard Stop** | **Red** | **DO NOT HIRE.** High liability risk. |

**Red Flags (Automatic Disqualification):**
*   [ ] EMR (Experience Modification Rate) > 1.2 for 2+ years.
*   [ ] No written safety manual/program.
*   [ ] Fatality on a jobsite in the last 3 years.
*   [ ] Refusal to provide Certificate of Insurance (COI).
