**1. SAFETY COMPLIANCE**
Contractor acknowledges that they have received, read, and understood the Company's Safety Rules and Contractor Orientation. Contractor agrees to abide by all applicable federal, state, and local safety regulations (including OSHA), as well as specific Company policies while on the premises.

**2. ASSUMPTION OF RISK**
Contractor understands that the work involves inherent risks, including but not limited to usage of heavy machinery, electrical hazards, and hazardous materials. Contractor hereby expressly and specifically assumes the risk of injury or harm in these activities.

**3. RELEASE AND WAIVER**
Contractor does hereby release and forever discharge and hold harmless the Company from any and all liability, claims, and demands of whatever kind or nature, either in law or in equity, which arise or may hereafter arise from Contractor's work at the Company's facility.

**4. INDEMNIFICATION**
Contractor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any and all claims, liabilities, judgments, settlements, damages, and costs (including attorney fees) arising out of the Contractor's negligence, willful misconduct, or failure to comply with safety regulations.

**5. INSURANCE**
Contractor confirms they maintain valid Workers' Compensation and General Liability insurance as specified in the Prequalification requirement and agrees to provide proof of such insurance upon request.

**6. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR**
It is understood that the Contractor is an independent entity and not an employee of the Company. Contractor is solely responsible for the safety, training, and supervision of its own employees.
