2. The 7 Wastes (TIMWOOD)

Show the facility photos with icons overlaid.

Transport Moving parts unnecessarily. (Forklift miles).
Inventory Storing parts "just in case". Hides problems.
Motion People moving (Walking, reaching, searching).
Waiting The silent killer. Machine running, operator watching.
Overproduction Making too much, too soon.
Overprocessing Doing more than the customer pays for.
Defects Scrap, rework, returns.
3. Technique #1: The Spaghetti Map

Goal: Visualize Motion Waste.

Have students draw their path for one cycle. If it looks like spaghetti, you are losing money.

Module 2: Execution
4. Technique #2: SMED (Rapid Changeover)

Concept: Externalize the internal.

Do 60% of the setup work while the machine is still running the previous job. Get tools ready, pre-heat dies, stage material.

5. Technique #3: Poka-Yoke (Mistake Proofing)

Concept: Make it impossible to fail.

  • Pins that only fit one way.
  • Color-coded bins.
  • Sensors that stop the line.
