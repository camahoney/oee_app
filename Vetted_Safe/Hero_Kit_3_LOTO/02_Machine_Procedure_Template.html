02. Shutdown & Isolation Sequence

1

Notify & Shutdown

Notify all affected operators. Press the "Stop" button on the Operator Control Panel. Wait for all motion to cease.

Photo: Control Panel
2

Isolate Electrical (E-1)

Locate Disconnect E-1 on the North Wall. Pull handle to "OFF". Apply Lock and Tag. Attempt to operate handle to verify it is locked.

Photo: Disconnect E-1
3

Isolate Pneumatic (P-1)

Turn yellow ball valve P-1 perpendicular to pipe. Open the bleed valve below to release stored pressure. Apply Lock and Tag.

Photo: Valve P-1

03. Verification (Tryout)

Action Required: 1. Ensure all personnel are clear.
2. Press the GREEN Start Button.
3. VERIFY machine does not start.
4. Return controls to OFF/Neutral.
