# Section A — Store Strategy Snapshot

## 1. Problem Framing: The "Safety Gap"
**Target Audience:** Small-to-Mid-Sized Enterprises (SMEs) with 20–250 employees (Construction, Manufacturing, Warehouse).
**The Pain Point:** They are too small to afford a full-time Safety Director ($100k+ salary) but face the *exact same* liability, OSHA fines, and audit risks as large corporations. They rely on an overworked Ops Manager or HR Generalist to "handle safety."
**The Solution:** Vetted Safe provides "Fractional Safety Director" value—resources that look and function like they came from a high-end consultancy, at a fraction of the cost. We sell **Confidence** and **Audit-Readiness**, not just templates.

## 2. Product Architecture (The Value Ladder)
We will structure the store to maximize Lifetime Value (LTV) through a logical upsell path.

*   **Tier 1: The "Band-Aid" (Entry \u003c $49)**
    *   *Goal:* Solve an immediate, bleeding pain point.
    *   *Examples:* Specific Checklists, Field Forms, Single Policy Templates.
    *   *Format:* Word/PDF.
*   **Tier 2: The "System" (Core $99 – $199)**
    *   *Goal:* Fix a broken process.
    *   *Examples:* Complete Training Modules (Deck + Quiz + Script), Program Manuals.
    *   *Format:* PPT + Word + Excel.
*   **Tier 3: The "Peace of Mind" (Premium $299+)**
    *   *Goal:* Total compliance for a major domain.
    *   *Examples:* Full Bundles (e.g., "The Contractor Gatekeeper System"), Implementation Kits.
    *   *Format:* Zip folder containing all file types + Video Guide.

## 3. Hero Kits (Top Revenue Drivers)
These are the "Flagship" products that will anchor the store and marketing.

1.  **The "Audit-Proof" Contractor Management Kit**
    *   *Why:* Contractor liability is the #1 sleepless night for owners.
    *   *Components:* Prequal form, legal-vetted waivers (template), orientation deck, badge system.
2.  **The "Day One" New Hire Orientation System**
    *   *Why:* Every company hires, and every bad accident happens in the first 6 months.
    *   *Components:* Universal safety induction deck, onboarding checklist, PPE sizing guide.
3.  **The "LOTO" (Lockout/Tagout) Zero-Energy Mastery Pack**
    *   *Why:* Consistently a Top 10 OSHA fine; highly technical and intimidating for non-experts.
    *   *Components:* Machine-specific procedure templates, annual audit tool, training deck.
4.  **The "Forklift Commander" Fleet Safety Bundle**
    *   *Why:* High frequency of incidents; certification is mandatory and expires every 3 years.
    *   *Components:* Certification tracker, practical evaluation checklist (road test), refresher training.
5.  **The "Safety Committee" Launchpad**
    *   *Why:* Many states require it; gives the Ops Manager a way to delegate safety tasks.
    *   *Components:* Meeting agenda templates, charter, minutes log, hazard hunt worksheets.
