Module 1: Core Safety Principles

01. Our Safety Culture

Production is NOT #1

If you can't do it safe, don't do it. We will never fire you for stopping a job because of a hazard.

Stop Work Authority

You have the authority and the duty to stop work if you see an imminent danger.

02. The "Cardinal Rules"

Violating these rules puts lives at risk and may result in immediate termination.

  • LOTO: Never bypass a lock or guard.
  • Heights: No working above 4ft without fall protection.
  • Confined Space: Never enter a tank/pit without a permit.
  • Substance Abuse: 100% Drug & Alcohol Free workplace (includes marijuana).

05. Bloodborne Pathogens

⚠
Universal Precaution: Treat ALL blood and bodily fluids as if they are infectious.
  • If a coworker is bleeding: Do NOT touch the blood. Call the First Response Team.
  • Cleanup: Only trained personnel (custodial/EHS) may clean up blood spills.
  • Exposure: If you are exposed, wash immediately and report it.

06. Emergency Action Plan

Fire / Evacuation

  1. Stop work safely.
  2. Walk to nearest Exit.
  3. Register at Rally Point.
  4. Wait for "All Clear".

Severe Weather

  1. Move to interior Shelter Area.
  2. Stay away from glass/windows.
  3. Crouch and cover head.

07. LOTO Awareness

You are an "Affected Employee".

  • ❌ You MAY NOT apply locks.
  • ❌ You MAY NOT remove locks.
  • ✅ You MUST respect the lock.

If a machine is locked out, it is being serviced. Attempting to start it could kill the mechanic inside.

08. Ergonomics & Lifting

Power Zone: Keep lifts between knees and shoulders.

  • Test the load before lifting.
  • Get help for anything over 50 lbs.
  • Don't twist your spine while holding a load. Turn your feet.

09. Incident Reporting

We want to know! Silence is the enemy of safety.

Near Miss

"Almost an accident." No one hurt. Report it so we can fix the hazard.

First Aid

Minor cut/scrape. Report it to get treatment and log it.

Lost Time

Serious injury. Requires immediate investigation.

10. Knowledge Check

Q1: Which color diamond represents Flammability?
