## Instructions
Watch the new employee perform their standard duties for 15–20 minutes. Do not intervene unless there is immediate danger. Check the boxes below.

### 1. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
*   [ ] **Eye Protection:** Safety glasses worn at all times? (Not on top of head).
*   [ ] **Footwear:** Proper boots laced up?
*   [ ] **Hearing Protection:** Earplugs properly inserted (rolled, not just resting)?
*   [ ] **Hand Protection:** Wearing gloves appropriate for the task?
*   [ ] **Condition:** Is PPE in good condition (not modified)?

### 2. Body Mechanics & Ergonomics
*   [ ] **Lifting:** Lifts with legs, keeps load close to body?
*   [ ] **Twisting:** Pivots feet instead of twisting spine?
*   [ ] **Awareness:** Looks before backing up or turning?
*   [ ] **Hands:** Keeps hands away from pinch points/line of fire?

### 3. Work Area & Housekeeping
*   [ ] **Organization:** Tools returned to proper place after use?
*   [ ] **Floors:** Area free of trip hazards (cords, trash)?
*   [ ] **Egress:** Path to fire exit is unblocked?
*   [ ] **Liquids:** Any spills wiped up immediately?

### 4. Tool & Equipment Use
*   [ ] **Inspection:** Did they inspect the tool before use?
*   [ ] **Guards:** All guards in place and functioning?
*   [ ] **Right Tool:** Using the correct tool for the job (not using a wrench as a hammer)?
*   [ ] **Cords:** Extension cords inspected for nicks/cuts?

### 5. Safety Culture Attitude
*   [ ] **Questions:** Did they ask for help when unsure?
*   [ ] **Pace:** Working at a safe, controlled speed (not rushing)?
*   [ ] **Focus:** Not distracted by phone or conversation?
