02. Replacement Policy

  • Wear & Tear: Using 1-for-1 exchange, we will replace worn out gear at no cost.
  • Lost/Negligence: Lost equipment will be replaced at the employee's expense (via payroll deduction where allowed by law).
  • Leaving Employment: All equipment must be returned upon termination or resignation.

Care & Maintenance
Topic: Equipment Life

How to Extend Equipment Life

Safety Glasses

Clean: Use lens wipes or mild soap and water. Never dry with dirty rag (scratches).

Replace When: Scratched, pitted, or bent frame.

Safety Boots

Clean: Brush off mud daily. Oil leather monthly.

Replace When: Steel toe is exposed, tread is smooth, or leather is cracked.

Hard Hats

Inspect: Check shell for cracks daily. Check suspension for fraying.

Expires: Replace suspension every 12 months. Replace shell every 5 years.

Respirators

Store: Must be stored in a sealed Ziploc bag when not in use to prevent filter saturation.

Replace: When breathing becomes difficult or you smell odors.

⚠
Safety Critical: Damaged PPE offers ZERO protection. If in doubt, throw it out and get a new one. Don't risk your eyes or toes to save the company $5.
